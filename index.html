What is Splace?

Splace is a combination of the word safe and place. We wanted to provide a comfortable setting for users to release anxiety digitally without the need of paper.

Can't you just write in a notebook?

The overconsumption of paper causes 14% of deforestations. Although the number seems minimal, 14% is very large in the perspective of deforestation. Our goal at Splace is to reduce paper consumption by creating personalized/customized E-Journals.

Why is Splace so great?

Instead of physical journals that people can often intrude, Splace will be secured with a login. We also want as much customization as possible to make it personal.
